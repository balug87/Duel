HOW TO PLAY

  1. Rest your cursor on the AMMO CYLINDER at the bottom-left and keep it there.
  2. Wait through the countdown — 3… 2… 1… Move off the cylinder early and the count resets!
  3. On FIRE!, click to shoot. Aiming is a straight line from the barrel through your cursor and beyond, so lead your shot rather than hovering exactly over the target. First to drop wins.
  4. Headshots are lethal. Body, arm and leg hits wear them down. You each get limited shots — if both stand when the smoke clears, it's a draw and you duel again.
  5. Score comes from draw speed, accuracy and remaining health. Beat every gunslinger to win.
